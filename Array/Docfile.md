1 Int= 4bytes
1 char= 1 bytes
1 double= 2 bytes
1 string = bytes

1000 bytes = 1 Kilobytes(KB)
1000 KB = 1 megabyte (MB)
1000MB = 1 Gigabyte(GB)
1000 GB = 1 Terabyte(TB)
1000 TB = 1 Petabyte(PB)
